### Dishari
*Dishari: AI-Powered Smart Navigation for the Visually Impaired*

**Subtotal: 13.5/15** (Presentation: — /5 → Final: — /20)

**1. Innovation & Originality — 5/5**
- On-device YOLOv5-nano with a 3×3 path grid mapped to tactile servo pop-ups is a distinctive assistive architecture, not a generic smart-cane clone.
- Avoids audio dependency for core feedback—clear originality in the interaction model.
- Strong technical specificity in both report and investment prospectus.

**2. Feasibility — 3.5/5**
- RPi + Pi Camera prototype path is concrete; BOM and institutional GTM are thought through.
- Still early: certification, field reliability outdoors, and manufacturing at $280–$380 retail remain hard.
- Seed ask ($180k–$250k) shows awareness of the gap between lab and product.

**3. Impact & Relevance — 5/5**
- Addresses independent mobility for BVI users in LMICs where premium ETAs are unaffordable.
- Institutional-first buyers (schools, NGOs, gov) match how assistive tech actually scales.
- Direct dignity, safety, and inclusion impact if the device works in real sidewalks.
