## Reasons

### 1. Innovation & Originality — 4/5
- Combines IoT sensing, CV disease detection, and GSM/IVR for feature-phone farmers—stronger than app-only agri tools.
- Paper sets hard constraints (off-grid, no data plan, BOM ৳8–12k).
- Not wholly new as a category, but the dual-mode (web + voice) packaging is well argued.

### 2. Feasibility — 3.5/5
- Lab prototype (ESP32, 72-hour bench trial) and tech stack are documented.
- Full farm lifecycle + aquaculture + AI offline on ESP32-S3 is ambitious for one team.
- Institution-pays / farmer-uses BM is realistic for Bangladesh.

### 3. Impact & Relevance — 5/5
- Targets >87% smallholders, water waste, and post-harvest/decision gaps central to BD agriculture.
- IVR path includes farmers without smartphones—high inclusion relevance.
- Clear SDG/food-security alignment from the materials.
