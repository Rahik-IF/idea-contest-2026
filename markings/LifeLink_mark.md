## Project snapshot (from submissions)

LifeLink AI is a QR medical ID bracelet linked to an encrypted cloud profile. Authorized responders see critical data (blood group, allergies, conditions, meds, contacts) plus an AI emergency summary. BM: sell hardware near cost, free basic profile, monetize via premium subscriptions and institutional/hospital-EMS licensing.

---

## 1. Innovation & Originality — 3/5

### What was evaluated
Novelty versus existing medical ID / ICE / QR health products.

### Analysis
QR/wearable medical IDs exist internationally. LifeLink’s AI summary and dual free-basic + institutional licensing BM are competent increments, not category creation. Documentation is polished; originality is moderate.

### Why this score
**3/5** — Useful increment on a known product class.

---

## 2. Feasibility — 4/5

### What was evaluated
Clarity of MVP, BM structure, and partnership path.

### Analysis
Scope is narrow and buildable (bracelet + cloud profile + auth scan + summary). Near-cost hardware + free basic + recurring B2B/B2C revenue is a coherent model. Privacy and EMS adoption are real work but within a student+partner horizon. One of the more executable health ideas.

### Why this score
**4/5** — Clear MVP and BM; adoption/privacy remain execution risks.

---

## 3. Impact & Relevance — 4.5/5

### What was evaluated
Emergency “golden hour” relevance when patients cannot speak.

### Analysis
Road accidents, chronic illness, allergies, elderly patients—highly relevant in BD. Free basic profile reduces exclusion. Impact is life-relevant if responders scan and trust data; slightly below Dishari/Solar Snow only because the wearable category is established and depends on ecosystem behavior.

### Why this score
**4.5/5** — Strong life-safety relevance with inclusive free tier.
