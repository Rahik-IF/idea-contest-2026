## Reasons

### 1. Innovation & Originality — 4.5/5
- Replacing interviews with a 3-day context-generated work simulation is a sharp product idea.
- Moment Replay + behavioral KPIs (latency, dual-pressure prioritization) go beyond generic AI screening.
- Clear differentiation from chat-based interview bots.

### 2. Feasibility — 4/5
- Live URL and end-to-end flow (connect tools → link → replay) are credible for a student build.
- SaaS pricing tiers are simple and coherent.
- Integrations (Slack/Jira/etc.) and enterprise trust remain the main execution risk—not idea vagueness.

### 3. Impact & Relevance — 3.5/5
- Bad-hire cost narrative is real for startups/tech hiring, but audience is narrower than mass BD problems.
- Strong B2B relevance; weaker broad social reach than agri/health/assistive entries.
- Useful if it reduces false positives in hiring; impact is organizational, not public-good first.
