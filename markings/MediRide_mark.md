## Reasons

### 1. Innovation & Originality — 2.5/5
- Nearest-ambulance + tracking is familiar (existing ambulance brands/apps named on their canvas).
- Extras (blood donor, event coverage, web SOS) are useful add-ons, not a new category.
- Low-moderate originality.

### 2. Feasibility — 3.5/5
- 15% trip commission and fare table are concrete; Rajshahi pilot GTM is sensible.
- Supply-side ambulance partnerships and 24/7 ops are the bottleneck.
- More feasible than full emergency OS, less than pure software tools.

### 3. Impact & Relevance — 4.5/5
- Ambulance delay in emergencies is a direct life-and-death BD pain point.
- Transparent pricing + tracking address trust and time.
- High relevance even if the product category is known.
