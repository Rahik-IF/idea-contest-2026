## Project snapshot (from submissions)

Ambulance marketplace with SOS, live GPS, transparent pricing, post-emergency follow-up, event coverage, blood donor and hospital info, web access. Revenue: 15% trip commission (fare table for 10 km / 20 km / inter-district), event fees, referrals, corporate contracts. Pilot GTM: Rajshahi ambulance associations.

### Analysis

### Why this score
**3.5/5** — Clear numbers and pilot plan; ops/supply risk remains.
