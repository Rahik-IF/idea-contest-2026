## Short Description (from submission form)

Kosh is a behavioral micro-savings platform that cures allowance leakage for Gen Z by transforming high-velocity mobile wallets into disciplined, goal-oriented savings vaults. Built specifically for university students, Kosh utilizes locked Capsules and a unique Strategic Friction model—including a ৳100 discipline fee for missed targets—to help users securely fund essential milestones like tuition and tech gear. By turning consistent daily micro-deposits into direct purchasing power through a dedicated Merchant Network, Kosh protects student capital while building a highly profitable, success-based fintech ecosystem.
