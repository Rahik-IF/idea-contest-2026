## Product
**CampusHub BD** — verified digital campus platform for students, universities, clubs, companies.

## Problem (Project Report)
University info scattered across Facebook, Messenger, WhatsApp, email, notice boards; missed announcements/events/internships; unorganized buying/selling, lost items, club activities.

## Solution (Project Report)
One verified platform with **role-based access**.

## Core modules / features cited (Project Report)
**Campus Feed** — timeline of academic/professional/extracurricular events; date-based timeline; official announcements; department-specific posts; categories/filters; search/bookmark; real-time notifications; national/international academic event updates.

## Revenue streams (Business Model)
- Featured job & internship posts
- Featured marketplace listings
- Company subscription plans (monthly/yearly)
- Course commission
- Sponsored events & promotions
- Premium university dashboard (analytics/management subscription)
- AI premium membership (resume review, career guidance, smart recommendations)
- Digital advertising
- Campus store commission
- Verification & premium seller services

**Future revenue opportunities listed:** Alumni network membership; scholarship & internship partner programs; event ticketing fees; API & SaaS for universities; privacy-compliant aggregated data analytics; corporate campus recruitment packages.

## Audiences (form)
Students, faculty, clubs, administration, companies, training providers, alumni.
