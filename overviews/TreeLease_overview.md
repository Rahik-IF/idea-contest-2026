> Compiled only from the contest CSV submission and the team's uploaded Business Model / Project Report files. No external judgment or invented claims.

## Short Description (from submission form)

TreeLease is a digital platform that allows people to rent a mango tree for an entire harvesting season directly from a registered orchard. Instead of purchasing mangoes from the market without knowing their origin or quality, customers can select a specific tree, reserve its seasonal harvest, and receive fresh mangoes through scheduled deliveries or by visiting the orchard during the harvesting period.

## Business Model (from BM PDF)

**Value proposition:** Reserve seasonal harvest of a specific mango tree from local orchards; fresh, traceable mangoes for consumers; farmers get advance payments and better market access.

**Customer segments:** Urban families; mango enthusiasts; corporate gifting; restaurants and cafés; Bangladeshi expatriates sending seasonal gifts.

**Revenue streams:**
- Seasonal tree reservation fees
- Commission from successful bookings
- Home delivery charges
- Premium services (priority delivery, orchard visits)

**Key partners:** Mango orchard owners; delivery providers; digital payment gateways; local agricultural organizations.

**Key activities:** Platform management; orchard partnerships; bookings/payments; delivery coordination; customer support.

**Cost structure:** Platform development & maintenance; marketing & customer acquisition; payment processing; delivery coordination; operational support.
