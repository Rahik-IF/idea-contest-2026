## Short Description (from submission form)

Bidhi is an open-access technological innovation explicitly engineered to dismantle this exploitative network. By providing a completely transparent, zero-fee digital bridge, Bidhi links clients directly with verified attorneys. Furthermore, to structurally prevent monetary influence from altering client recommendations within the software, the platform enforces a strict, single flat-rate subscription model for all listed lawyers. This digital environment ensures that attorney visibility remains entirely merit-based rather than wealth-dependent.

## Product (Report + form)
**BIDHI** — open-access platform linking clients directly to verified attorneys; **zero-fee** for public search; lawyers on a **single flat-rate subscription** so visibility is merit-based (not pay-to-rank).

## Problem context (Project Report)
Three systemic issues named:
1. Socio-economic extortion via unregulated middlemen finders’ fees
2. Asymmetric quality control (brokers funnel to highest kickback, not best fit)
3. Lawyers paying substantial commission to brokers

## Technical approach (Project Report)
Monolithic web app on self-managed VPS: **Django (Python)** + **MySQL**.

## Monetization (Project Report — not the empty BM docx)
| Dimension | Free baseline | Professional subscription |
|-----------|---------------|---------------------------|
| Audience | General public / litigants | Verified legal practitioners |
| Cost | 100% free forever | Single flat rate (example given: **500 BDT/month**) |
| Capabilities | Knowledge base, lawyer search, reviews | Full profile viewability, scheduling, direct inquiry messaging |
| Visibility boost | Impossible to buy rank | Flat-rate equality model (rejects pay-per-lead / sponsored placement) |

## Business plan stance (Project Report)
Rejects pay-per-lead / sponsored placements / commission billing as digitizing the broker problem; operates under **Flat-Rate Equality Business Model**.
