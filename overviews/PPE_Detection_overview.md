## Short Description (from submission form)

Workplace safety is a critical concern across high-risk industries such as chemical processing, construction, manufacturing, and shipbuilding. These environments often involve hazardous operations where strict compliance with Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) regulations is essential to prevent serious injuries and fatalities.
However, current PPE monitoring practices are largely manual, making them inconsistent, labor-intensive, and highly susceptible to human error. In large-scale or fast-paced industrial settings, ensuring 100% compliance through manual supervision becomes impractical. Moreover, traditional safety checks typically lack real-time responsiveness, comprehensive data logging, and scalability.
To address these limitations, this project proposes the development of an AI Based PPE Detection System. The system is designed to automatically identify critical PPE components such as helmets, gloves, masks, and safety vests on workers in real time. It will also be capable of issuing instant alerts for non-compliance and generating actionable compliance reports to assist safety officers in enforcing safety standards efficiently.
This solution not only enhances real-time safety monitoring but also promotes a proactive safety culture by providing data-driven insights for continuous improvement. Given the increasing emphasis on smart industry practices and workplace safety, this project idea is both timely and highly relevant to modern industrial needs.
