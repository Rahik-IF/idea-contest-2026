## Short Description (from submission form)

বৈদ্য বাড়ি (BOIDDOBARI) is an AI-powered healthcare navigation platform designed to simplify the healthcare journey for patients. It helps users analyze symptoms, identify the right medical specialist, and find nearby hospitals based on their healthcare needs. The platform also enables online appointment booking, ambulance service requests, and AI-powered health assistance through an intelligent chatbot. Users can securely manage their digital health records and receive personalized health guidance in one place. By integrating AI, healthcare providers, and emergency services into a single ecosystem, বৈদ্য বাড়ি aims to make healthcare more accessible, efficient, and patient-centered while reducing delays in treatment and improving overall healthcare experiences.
