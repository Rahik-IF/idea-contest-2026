## Short Description (from submission form)

SkillMatch AI is an intelligent career mentorship platform designed to bridge the gap between university education and industry requirements. The platform uses Artificial Intelligence to assess students' skills through CV analysis, technical assessments, and AI-powered mock interviews. Based on each student's strengths, weaknesses, and current job market trends, it generates personalized learning roadmaps to help them develop in-demand skills.

The platform also connects students with verified internships and employment opportunities, creating a seamless pathway from learning to employment. By providing accessible, personalized, and affordable career guidance, SkillMatch AI empowers students to improve their employability while helping employers discover skilled, job-ready talent. The solution aims to reduce graduate unemployment, strengthen industry–academia collaboration, and contribute to sustainable economic growth.
