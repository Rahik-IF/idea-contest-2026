## Short Description (from submission form)

LifeLink AI is an AI-powered emergency medical identity system designed to provide instant access to a patient's critical health information during medical emergencies. Using a secure QR-code-enabled wearable bracelet, authorized responders can quickly retrieve essential details such as blood group, allergies, medical conditions, medications, and emergency contacts. The system also generates an AI-powered emergency summary that highlights the most critical information, enabling faster, more informed medical decisions while protecting patient privacy through encrypted cloud storage and secure access controls.

## Product (Project Report)
**LifeLink AI** — wearable medical bracelet with one encrypted QR linked to secure cloud profile; authorized scan returns critical medical info + **AI-generated emergency summary**. Goal stated: reduce treatment delay and improve survival in emergencies. Explicitly does **not** replace medical expertise — removes bottleneck of accessing patient history when the patient cannot communicate.
