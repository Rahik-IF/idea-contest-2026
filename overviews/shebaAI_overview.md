## Problem (from Project Report)
Four frustrations when hiring local specialists:
1. No trust signal (rarely verified ID/rating/track record)
2. Opaque on-the-spot pricing / inflated “emergency” charges
3. No payment protection (cash, no escrow)
4. Slow manual matching (neighbours, Facebook, cold calls; no ETA/urgency triage)
