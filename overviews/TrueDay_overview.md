## Short Description (from submission form)

TrueDay is an AI-powered hiring platform that replaces traditional interviews with realistic, 3-day work simulations. By auto-generating scenarios based on real company context, TrueDay measures invisible behavioral character—like response latency and prioritization under pressure—allowing hiring managers to test-drive candidates and eliminate the costly blind spot of a bad hire.

## Problem stats cited (slides)
74% employers admit bad hire; avg direct cost **$17,000**; 89% hiring failures culture fit not skill.

## How it works (slides)
Connect Slack, Jira, GitHub, Figma, Google Workspace → AI auto-generates scenario → one link to candidate → Moment Replay + Debrief. Day sessions ~20–40 minutes as shown.

## Behavioral KPIs tracked (slides)
Response latency; selective attention; communication shifts under stress; follow-through; tab-switching/dwell/prioritization under dual pressure.
