## Short Description (from submission form)

ResQ Plus is an AI-powered emergency response and coordination platform designed to improve emergency management in Bangladesh. It connects citizens, hospitals, ambulance services, volunteers, and family members through a unified intelligent system. By enabling real-time coordination, smart SOS, AI-assisted decision support, and location-based emergency response, ResQ Plus aims to reduce response time, improve communication, and help save more lives during critical situations.

## Product
**ResQ Plus** — AI emergency response & coordination layer for Bangladesh. Does not replace emergency services — connects citizens, volunteers, hospitals, ambulances, families.

## Core problem (Presentation)
“Emergency services exist. Coordination doesn't.” Delays in triage, routing, notification; ambulance delays; limited first-aid knowledge; hospital confusion.

## MVP features (Presentation)
Smart SOS (one-tap + auto-priority); volunteer network; AI emergency assistant (triage/next steps); emergency contact notification; live GPS tracking; hospital recommendation (nearest capable facility).
